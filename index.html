How it works

SET UP ELECTION

On successful registration, with our effortless ballot setup, you can set-up election in no time. This process involves 3 steps:
1. Create an account for admin.
2. Add voters' and candidate list.
3. Create Unique Election ID

VERIFICATION

Voter's & Candidates can register on Suffragio and verify their name and details updated by admin.

VOTING

After 3 step verification process, voters can record their vote through any internet-enabled device. 3 step verification including your login ID and password, OTP to registered mail and biometric verification.

RESULTS

After the election ends, on approval of election officer, the result can be generated which is automatically tabulated.
